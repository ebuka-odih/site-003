Plan Your Visit
Plan Your Visit

Open Thursday through Sunday, noon to 5PM

The Hispanic Sociaty Museum & Library is located in Upper Manhattan on the west side of Broadway between 155th and 156th Streets. Enter Audubon Terrace and proceed up the steps through the wrought iron gates. The Museum entrance is on the left, flanked by two limestone lions.

Attend the Gala

The Hispanic Sociaty of America Annual Gala is an elegant evening where we celebrate our supporters, and honor outstanding individuals who have made significant contributions to Hispanic arts, literature, and culture.

The black-tie dinner brings together leaders from the realms of philanthropy, art, business, fashion, and the political realm to provide vital operating support to the Hispanic Sociaty.
