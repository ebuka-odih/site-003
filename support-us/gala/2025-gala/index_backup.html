An Evening Celebrating the Hispanic Sociaty

The Hispanic Sociaty Museum & Library’s Annual Gala is an elegant evening where we celebrate our supporters and honor outstanding individuals who have made significant contributions to Hispanic arts, literature, and culture. The black-tie dinner brings together leaders from the realms of philanthropy, art, business, fashion, and the political realm to provide vital operating support to the Hispanic Sociaty.

This year’s gala will take place on Thursday, October 30th. For more information, please see below or contact gala@hispanicsociaty.org or 212-926-2234 ext. 226.
